Hostel life

Hostel life is meant to be a lot of fun and you live it the right way, you will cherish those memories for a lifetime. The group studies! I owe half my marks to roommates and friends.Every hostelite irrespective of his or her experience will remember the combined study sessions or at least the good times spent in the name of study ! The good thing about hostels is that you have to live with all kinds of people and you have to communicate with almost all of them. Loud music, party all night after the exam is over , hearty laughs, heated arguments on debated topics followed by endless bonding are part of just another average day in life of a hostelite.
